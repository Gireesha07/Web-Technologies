STORY EXPLANATION

"Hi Nanna" is an emotional family drama that revolves around a single father (Nani) and his daughter. Their lives take a turn when a woman (Mrunal Thakur) enters their world, bringing warmth, love, and emotional depth. The story beautifully explores relationships, parenthood, and unconditional love.

TECHNICAL ASPECTS

Cinematography: Rich visuals with beautiful natural frames.

Editing: Smooth transitions and well-paced scenes.

Music & BGM: Soulful songs and heart-touching background score.

Screenplay: Emotional and engaging.

Acting: Natural performances by all actors.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

Feedback: The movie is heartwarming and emotional with strong family values.

Drawback: Slightly slow in a few parts, but overall impactful.
